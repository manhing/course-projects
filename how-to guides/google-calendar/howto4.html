Direct link to create an event

A great way to advertise an event is with a direct link on a company's website. Users can simply click a hyperlink and are just one more click away from having an event added to their own calendar.

Here is a sample template: 

https://calendar.google.com/calendar/render?action=TEMPLATE&text=Sample+Event+Name&dates=20160527T124007/20160527T131507&details=text+for+details&location=Googleplex,+1600+Amphitheatre+Pkwy,+Mountain+View,+CA+94043#eventpage_6

Most of the values entered into the URL is self-explanatory except maybe the time zone. The 07 at the end of each dateTime denotes PDT. 

dates=20160527T124007/20160527T131507
